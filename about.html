Our Story

In April 2019, a team of Lambda School students came together during the schools Build Week to scope out the blueprints of what would become GlucosIQ. With each team member having some kind of personal link to somebody living with the disease, it became apparent early on that a better solution to managing the disease needed to exist. The team set out to provide a data science solution to helping those with diabetes better understand their glucose data in order to transform their daily lifestyle decisions.


Since then, GlucosIQ has gone on to become an innovative international company with offices in San Francisco, Chicago, and London. The team prides itself in creating beautifully designed, functional software that delights all our users around the World.
